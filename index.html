About AIMEX

AIMEX is a National Level Technical Fest organized by the Department of CSE (AI & ML) at MITS, where Artificial Intelligence meets innovation, creativity, and technology.

About the Department
At MITS, Deemed to be University, the Department of CSE (AI and ML) is committed to nurturing future-ready engineers with strong technical and analytical skills. The program focuses on building a solid foundation in AI, ML, Data Science, Neural Networks, and Deep Learning. Students gain hands-on experience through advanced laboratories, real-time projects, internships, and industry-oriented curriculum.

About Our College
Madanapalle Institute of Technology and Science (MITS) is a prestigious Deemed to be University located in Madanapalle, Andhra Pradesh. The institution is recognized for its academic excellence, innovation-driven education, and strong research culture. MITS offers a wide range of undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs in engineering, technology, management, and sciences. The university follows an outcome-based education system with a strong emphasis on practical learning and industry relevance. It is equipped with state-of-the-art infrastructure, advanced laboratories, smart classrooms, and modern research facilities. MITS has a highly qualified and experienced faculty who guide students toward academic and professional excellence.
