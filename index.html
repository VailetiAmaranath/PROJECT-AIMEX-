About Aimex

AIMEX is the flagship technical fest of the CSE (AI and ML) Department, designed to ignite innovation, creativity, and technical excellence. The fest serves as a dynamic platform where technology meets imagination, bringing together students, innovators, and future technologists under one roof. AIMEX features a wide range of technical events including hackathons, coding challenges, AI model building, data science contests, and project expos that encourage real-time problem solving.

About the Department

At MITS, Deemed to be University, the Department of CSE (AI and ML) is committed to nurturing future-ready engineers with strong technical and analytical skills. The program focuses on building a solid foundation in AI, ML, Data Science, Neural Networks, and Deep Learning. Students gain hands-on experience through advanced laboratories, real-time projects, internships, and industry-oriented curriculum.

About the College

Madanapalle Institute of Technology and Science (MITS) is a prestigious Deemed to be University located in Madanapalle, Andhra Pradesh. The institution is recognized for its academic excellence, innovation-driven education, and strong research culture. MITS offers a wide range of undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs in engineering, technology, management, and sciences. The university follows an outcome-based education system with a strong emphasis on practical learning and industry relevance.
